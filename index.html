Birth - Childhood

23rd, December/2004

On this date, I was born at the CHUK hospital in Kacyiru. Born from my wonderful mother, UMUHOZA Fatuma, I was named Gitori Ismael. I quickly built up a burning passion for dancing in my childhood. American dance specifically.

Starting school

Early 2010

I did not start school from nursery like many of my fellows. I jumped straight to primary one at KARUGIRA Primary School. I left that school after three years and moved to La Promise school in Rubavu where I graduated primary school.

High School

January, 2018

I was excited of going to High School. It gave me more confidence and a feeling of growth. I studied Junior high school at Aim High Gatenga School and graduated junior high school there.

A' level - Present

September, 2021

O'level was done and things got more serious. I chose to specialise in Math, Physics and Computer Science(MPC). I am still studying this combination at A.P.E Rugunga in Kiyovu.
